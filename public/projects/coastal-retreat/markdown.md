## Coastal Retreat

A resilient and elegant seaside home designed to withstand high-salinity air and extreme weather.

### Project Details
Building on the coast presents unique challenges. We used marine-grade concrete and specialized protective coatings while maintaining a light, airy aesthetic characterized by bleached oak and white stucco.

*   **Completion Time**: 11 Months
*   **Key Features**: Wrap-around decks, wind-resistant glazing, solar array.
*   **Contract Type**: Custom Build Contract
