## Urban Penthouse Loft

A complex renovation and extension of a historical industrial building into a luxury living space.

### Project Details
This project required significant structural reinforcement to support the addition of a rooftop garden and private pool. The interior design features raw concrete, steel beams, and custom walnut cabinetry.

*   **Completion Time**: 9 Months
*   **Key Features**: Rooftop oasis, custom steel staircase, hidden library.
*   **Contract Type**: Structural Renovation
