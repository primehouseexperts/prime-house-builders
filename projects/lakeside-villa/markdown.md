## Modern Lakeside Villa

Located on the serene shores of Lake Geneva, this project involved the construction of a 5,000 sq. ft. sustainable luxury home.

### Project Details
The client requested a design that blurred the lines between indoor and outdoor living. We utilized floor-to-ceiling glass panels and natural stone cladding to harmonize the structure with its environment.

*   **Completion Time**: 14 Months
*   **Key Features**: Infinity pool, smart-home integration, geothermal heating.
*   **Contract Type**: Full Turnkey Build
